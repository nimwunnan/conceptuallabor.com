The Theory of Conceptual Labor describes the ways that one must work when the job one is doing cannot be easily explained or understood.

This website contains the full text of the Theory, a bibliography of cited sources and further reading, and a blog where supplemental essays, links, and events will be posted infrequenly but regularly.
